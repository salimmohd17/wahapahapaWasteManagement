Our Services

At Wahapahapa Waste Management, we are committed to providing comprehensive waste management solutions tailored to the needs of our community. Here are the key services we offer:

Residential Waste Collection

We provide efficient and reliable waste collection services for households, ensuring that waste is disposed of responsibly and on a regular schedule.

Commercial Waste Management

Our commercial waste management services cater to businesses of all sizes, offering customized solutions to manage waste effectively and sustainably.

Recycling Programs

We promote sustainability through our recycling programs, helping the community reduce waste by recycling various materials and educating residents on best practices.

Hazardous Waste Disposal

Wahapahapa Waste Management offers safe and compliant disposal of hazardous waste, ensuring that dangerous materials are handled and disposed of properly to protect the environment.
