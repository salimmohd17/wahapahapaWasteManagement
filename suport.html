What We Do

Daily Waste Collection

Coordinating garbage pickups in Kombani, Maganyakulo, and Tiwi. Involving youth and riders for effective operations.

Drainage & Street Cleaning

We prevent flooding and promote hygiene by unblocking drains and cleaning public walkways regularly.

Beautification Projects

Greening and uplifting public spaces through tree planting, flower beds, and murals.

Community Engagement

Organizing clean-up competitions, educating schools, and working with municipal governments.
