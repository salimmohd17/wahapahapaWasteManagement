OUR ACTIVITIES

Wahapahapa Waste Management focuses on community-driven solutions to urban waste challenges. Through a blend of hands-on cleanup efforts, education, and advocacy, we aim to make our towns cleaner and greener while empowering local residents.

Daily Waste Collection

Coordinating early morning garbage pickup in Kombani, Maganyakulo, and Tiwi, engaging youth and riders in efficient collection.

Street & Drain Cleaning

Clearing blocked drainage systems and sweeping streets to prevent flooding and ensure clean, safe walkways.

Beautification Projects

Transforming neglected areas into attractive green zones with flowers, art, and community-led landscaping.

Community Competitions

Hosting clean-up contests for bodaboda stages and youth groups to foster pride and consistency in cleanliness.

Environmental Education

Running awareness campaigns and school workshops on responsible waste disposal, recycling, and reuse.

Municipal Collaborations

Partnering with local authorities to align efforts in sustainable urban planning and policy development.

Recycling Initiatives

Daily sorting and recycling of collected waste materials, reducing environmental pollution, and promoting sustainable practices.

Community Clean-ups

Volunteers engage in routine clean-up activities in neighborhoods, markets, schools, and public spaces, ensuring a cleaner environment for all.

Tree Planting & Beautification

Collaborating with schools and local institutions to plant trees and improve public spaces through green initiatives.

Education & Awareness

Daily workshops and seminars to educate communities on proper waste management practices, the importance of recycling, and environmental conservation.

Market Waste Management

Assisting market vendors in handling waste efficiently, providing collection bins, and creating awareness on proper disposal methods.

Join Us in Making a Difference!

Our efforts are sustained by the dedication of volunteers and the generosity of donors. Your support helps us continue this vital work. Whether through a donation or your time, you can help create a cleaner, healthier future for everyone.
