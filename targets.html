Our Key Targets

1. Zero Waste to Landfill

By 2030, our goal is to divert 100% of waste from landfills through recycling, composting, and waste-to-energy processes. This will not only reduce environmental impact but also create sustainable solutions for managing waste in our communities.

2. Community Engagement

We aim to actively engage with local communities by organizing 50+ voluntary cleanup initiatives each year. Our focus is to raise awareness, educate residents on waste reduction, and foster a culture of responsible waste management.

3. Clean Energy Integration

Wahapahapa Waste Management seeks to integrate clean energy sources into waste processing. Our target is to generate 20% of our operational energy from renewable sources by 2026, reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainability.

4. Plastic Reduction Program

We aim to reduce plastic waste by 40% over the next five years. Through partnerships with local businesses and government, we promote the use of alternatives like biodegradable materials and reusable packaging.

5. Safe Disposal and Recycling Facilities

Our goal is to establish certified recycling and safe disposal facilities in all major towns by 2025. These facilities will provide proper waste processing and create jobs within the local economy.
