WAHAPAHAPA WASTE MANAGEMENT

Wahapahapa Waste Management was established in 2021 in response to growing concerns about uncollected waste, blocked drainage systems, and environmental degradation in KWALE COUNTY. Our mission is to provide sustainable, community-driven solutions to local waste challenges while inspiring environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To become a leading catalyst for sustainable waste management and environmental protection, empowering communities to create cleaner, greener, and more resilient environments.

Our Mission

Our mission is to engage, educate, and empower communities to adopt sustainable waste management practices through innovative solutions, partnerships, and inclusive participation, ensuring a healthier environment for future generations.

The State of the Problem

In our neighborhoods, waste often goes uncollected for days, causing health hazards, flooding from blocked drainage, and polluted public spaces. Informal dumping is common, and most households lack awareness on proper waste disposal or recycling. Globally, over 2 billion tons of solid waste are generated annually, with a large percentage ending up in landfills or the ocean. This situation demands urgent, coordinated action.

Our Initiatives

Our daily and weekly initiatives include street and market cleanups, drainage unclogging, beautification through tree planting, and public sensitization. We work closely with youth groups, bodaboda riders, schools, and local authorities to make these efforts inclusive and consistent. We also promote income-generating activities through recycling, encouraging reuse, and upcycling waste into valuable products.
