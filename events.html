Ongoing Activities

Daily Sweeping

Our teams conduct early morning sweeping across markets, streets, and estates to maintain a clean and healthy environment.

Skip Bin Management

We regularly collect and manage skip bins around Kombani, Tiwi, and Maganyakulo to prevent waste overflow and pollution.

Slashing & Beautification

Our youth teams trim overgrown grass and beautify roadsides, making our towns cleaner, safer, and greener for all.

Community Awareness

We engage locals through one-on-one awareness and school programs promoting sustainable waste habits.
